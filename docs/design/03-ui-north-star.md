# 03 — UI North Star

This document is the single source of truth for page structure, menu groups, and major interactions.

## UX Rules

- Map-first on desktop, task-first on mobile.
- One clear primary action per surface.
- Every critical warning includes direct fix actions.
- Keep terminology action-oriented: Assign, Import, Upgrade, Optimize, Automate.

## Page Specs

### Command (Mission Control)
- **Purpose:** live operations overview and intervention.
- **Menu groups:** top economy strip, bottleneck panel, live orbital map, context panel, quick-action strip.
- **Core actions:** `Assign Ship`, `Create Route`, `Buy Fuel`, `Resolve Alert`.

### Survey
- **Purpose:** discover and rank opportunities.
- **Menu groups:** scan viewport, candidates, probe controls, discovery feed.
- **Core actions:** `Launch Probe`, `Focus Scan`, `Commit Survey`, `Bookmark Prospect`.

### Fleet & Routes
- **Purpose:** keep transport network efficient.
- **Menu groups:** fleet roster, route timeline, template queue, readiness/maintenance.
- **Core actions:** `Buy Ship`, `Plan Route`, `Assign Cargo`, `Set Repeat Route`, `Optimize Fuel`.

### Industry
- **Purpose:** maintain throughput and unblock chains.
- **Menu groups:** chain graph, facility cards, buffers, shortage diagnosis.
- **Core actions:** `Build Facility`, `Set Recipe`, `Upgrade Tier`, `Prioritize Output`, `Request Import`.

### Colonies
- **Purpose:** sustain life support and unlock growth tiers.
- **Menu groups:** population header, life support meters, upgrade checklist, risk trend.
- **Core actions:** `Import Essentials`, `Allocate Workforce`, `Start Upgrade`, `Set Emergency Protocol`.

### Market
- **Purpose:** stabilize cashflow and cover supply gaps.
- **Menu groups:** buy/sell board, contracts, budget forecast.
- **Core actions:** `Sell Batch`, `Buy Materials`, `Accept Contract`.

### Research & Automation
- **Purpose:** unlock leverage and reduce repetitive actions.
- **Menu groups:** research lanes, rules list, ROI panel.
- **Core actions:** `Start Research`, `Queue Research`, `Create Rule`, `Enable/Disable Automation`.

## Cross-Page Alert Ladder (Standardized)

1. Detect
2. Diagnose
3. Act
4. Verify

Every critical issue should follow this same interaction model.
