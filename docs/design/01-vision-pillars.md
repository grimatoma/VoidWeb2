# 01 — Vision & Pillars

## One-Sentence Vision

**Void Yield 2** is an optimistic hard-sci-fi, browser-based incremental logistics game where the player builds a space industry network from a startup operation into a self-sustaining inter-orbital corporation.

## Experience Promise

Players should feel like they are operating a serious orbital logistics command center where routes, timing, fuel, and colony support all matter—but remain readable and manageable.

## Core Pillars

1. **Orbital Logistics Is the Signature System**
   - Routes, transfer windows, and moving orbital bodies create meaningful planning choices.
2. **Colonies Are Power With Obligations**
   - Population unlocks people capacity and growth leverage, but requires reliable life support and advanced goods.
3. **Incremental Growth With Real Bottlenecks**
   - Storage, shipping, fuel, workforce, and production dependencies shape decisions.
4. **Readable Hard Sci-Fi**
   - Scientific flavor is grounded, but all gameplay implications are clear in UI.
5. **Stable Economy (No Price Speculation)**
   - Market behavior supports planning and logistics, not day-trading gameplay.
6. **AFK-Friendly, Not Punishing**
   - Progress continues while away; failures should be recoverable, not catastrophic.

## Visual Tone

- **Primary mood:** NASA-industrial orbital command.
- **Centerpiece:** dark orbital map with practical management panels.
- **Secondary tone:** optimistic engineering progress over dystopian collapse.
