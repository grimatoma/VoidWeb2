# 02 — Core Loops & Systems

## Primary Gameplay Loop

1. **Survey prospects** and reveal value signals.
2. **Establish extraction** on viable targets.
3. **Move materials** through constrained routes.
4. **Process materials** into increasingly specialized outputs.
5. **Support colony needs** to unlock higher people capacity.
6. **Reinvest into fleet/research/automation** to reduce manual overhead.

## Secondary Loops

### Logistics Loop
- Select cargo, route, ship, and departure timing.
- Balance ETA vs fuel efficiency vs delivery urgency.
- Detect and recover from congestion, idle ships, or missed windows.

### Industry Loop
- Convert raw resources through facility chains.
- Prioritize constrained outputs based on immediate bottlenecks.
- Expand from early ore->metals into colony-required advanced goods.

### Colony Loop
- Maintain water/oxygen/food/spares stability.
- Meet tier-up material requirements.
- Convert colony upgrades into workforce and strategic capability.

### Automation Loop
- Unlock research.
- Add condition->action rules for routine operations.
- Shift player role from manual dispatcher to system planner.

## Economy Constraints

- Earth market should provide stable buy/sell anchors.
- Contracts should reward operational consistency, not speculative timing.
- Monetary pressure should encourage better logistics and production decisions.

## Failure Design

- Warnings should trigger before hard stops.
- Shortages should reduce growth/efficiency first, then pause systems.
- Recovery paths must be explicit and fast to execute.
